Why the Highlands is Different

Two very different markets — tourists and locals — both searching online

The Southern Highlands has a unique dual economy. Weekend visitors from Sydney drive enormous demand for accommodation, dining and experiences. Locals and the growing permanent population need trades, health, professional services and retail. Your website needs to speak to both.

🏭 Bowral

The commercial heart. Highest foot traffic, strongest tourism pull, most competitive search results.

🏭 Mittagong

Gateway to the Highlands. Strong local trade base, growing residential population, less digital competition.

🏭 Moss Vale

Service hub for the LGA. Professional services, health and trades serving a wide regional catchment.

🌿 Berrima

Heritage tourism destination. Boutique retail, hospitality and artisan businesses with high visitor intent.

🌿 Bundanoon

Scenic village with strong tourism. Cafés, B&Bs and wellness businesses competing for weekend visitors.

🏭 Welby & Braemar

Growing residential areas. Local service businesses with an underserved online market.
