Our story

Built by someone who managed 600+ clients at a national agency — and left to do it better

Before founding Wollongong Web Design Co, I was second-in-command at one of Australia's larger digital agencies — overseeing a portfolio of more than 600 clients across every industry and market segment. I led teams, set strategy, and watched from the inside as large agencies scaled their way to mediocrity: more clients, thinner attention, worse results.

Across those 600 businesses, I kept seeing the same pattern. The best local operators — the ones with genuine reputations, loyal customers and real expertise — were consistently losing online to inferior competitors who simply had a better website and a smarter Google presence. Not because the big agency couldn't fix it. Because no one was paying close enough attention.

I moved to Wollongong and saw the same gap in the Illawarra. Brilliant local businesses invisible on Google. Tradies losing jobs to competitors with worse work and better SEO. Restaurants packed on Saturday, quiet on Tuesday, with no digital system to change that. It's a solvable problem — and solving it is exactly what I built this agency to do.

Wollongong Web Design Co takes on a limited number of clients so every business gets the level of attention I always wanted to give but couldn't at scale. You deal directly with me — the person who built the strategy, writes the copy, manages the SEO and knows your business. No account managers. No offshore handoffs. Just results.
