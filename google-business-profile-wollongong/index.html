The problem

Why most Wollongong businesses are invisible on Google Maps

A Google Business Profile isn't "set and forget." The businesses ranking in the 3-pack are actively managing theirs every week.

🔒

Unclaimed or incomplete profile

An unclaimed GBP has wrong hours, missing photos and no business description. Google treats it as untrustworthy — and so do customers.

⭐

Too few reviews (or too many bad ones)

Review volume and rating are two of Google's top local ranking factors. Competitors with 50+ reviews consistently outrank businesses with 5 — regardless of service quality.

📍

Wrong category or service area

Choosing the wrong primary category or not listing service areas is one of the most common — and most damaging — GBP mistakes. You simply won't appear for the right searches.

📷

No photos or outdated images

Profiles with 100+ photos get 520% more calls than profiles with no photos. Most businesses upload a logo and stop. That's leaving serious leads on the table.

📋

No GBP posts or updates

Weekly Google Posts signal to Google that your business is active. Competitors who post regularly edge out dormant profiles — even if the dormant business is more established.

⚠

NAP inconsistency across the web

If your business name, address or phone number differs between your website, GBP, and local directories, Google loses trust in your data and your ranking suffers for it.
