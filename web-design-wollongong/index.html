The Hard Truth

Why Most Wollongong Business Websites Don't Deliver Results

Here's what most web agencies won't tell you: a beautiful website that nobody finds is just an expensive business card. And most Wollongong business websites — even recent ones — fall into exactly this trap.

We audit a lot of sites. The same problems come up every time. If your website isn't generating enquiries, there's a good chance one of these is the reason.

🐌

Too slow on mobile

Over 70% of searches in the Illawarra happen on a phone. A site that takes more than 3 seconds to load on 4G loses most of its visitors before the page even renders — and Google ranks slow sites lower.

📄

Built on a bloated template

Cheap template-based websites look fine at first glance, but Google can spot the bloated code, duplicate structure, and generic content. They rank accordingly — which is to say, they don't.

📍

No local SEO signals

A Wollongong website that doesn't include suburb references, local schema, or service area signals gives Google no reason to show it for local searches. Your competitors appear in the map pack while you sit on page four.

🎯

No conversion strategy

Getting visitors to your site is only half the problem. If your website doesn't guide them toward a phone call, an enquiry form, or a purchase — it's not working for your business.

🕰️

Set up and forgotten

Websites need ongoing attention — security updates, speed optimisation, content improvements, and SEO refinement. A site built and abandoned in 2021 is losing ground to competitors every single month.

Our Approach

Wollongong Web Design Built for Google From Day One

Most agencies design a beautiful website and then ask "should we do SEO?" We flip that. Every website we build starts with search strategy — because a site that doesn't rank is a missed opportunity every single day.

🧹

Clean, semantic code

Every website we build uses clean HTML, properly structured heading hierarchies, and semantic markup that Google can crawl and understand. No bloated page builders, no unnecessary plugins, no render-blocking scripts.

📱

Mobile-first design

We design for mobile screens first, then scale up to desktop — because that's how Google evaluates your site. Every breakpoint is tested, every tap target is correctly sized, every font is readable without zooming.

Google's Core Web Vitals — Largest Contentful Paint, Cumulative Layout Shift, First Input Delay — are direct ranking factors. We optimise images, fonts, and scripts at build time so your site passes these benchmarks from launch day.

📍

Local SEO signals baked in

We structure your content, metadata, and schema markup to signal Wollongong relevance to Google from the moment your site goes live — so you're not starting from scratch after launch.

🗂️

Schema markup standard

LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList — we add structured data to every page so Google can understand your content and display it as rich results in search.

🇦🇺

Fast Australian hosting

Every website we host runs on Australian servers with CDN, SSL, daily backups, and uptime monitoring. Fast load times for Wollongong customers — and a local hosting signal Google rewards in Australian search results.

How It Works

How We Build Your Wollongong Website — From First Call to Launch

We've refined our web design process over 15 years and 165+ Wollongong websites. Transparent, structured, and designed to get you live with a website you're genuinely proud of — on time and on budget.

1

Discovery & Strategy Session

Every project starts with a free 30-minute consultation — by phone, Zoom, or in person at our North Wollongong studio. We cover your business and goals, your target customers, your competitors, your current website (if you have one), your budget, and your timeline. From that conversation we build a detailed project brief and scope of works that both parties sign off on before anything begins. No scope creep, no surprises — just a clear plan you can hold us to.

2

Sitemap, Wireframes & Content Planning

Before any design begins, we map out the full site structure — every page, its purpose, and how it connects to others. We identify the keywords each page should target and advise on the content you'll need to provide (or we can write it for you — our copywriting service is available for all projects). Wireframes come next: low-fidelity layouts that establish the structure and flow of each page before visual design begins. This step saves revision time downstream and ensures the design serves the content, not the other way around.

3

Design Concept & Approval

We design a full homepage concept in desktop and mobile views, incorporating your branding, colour palette, typography, and photography. You receive two rounds of revisions included in your project fee. We present the design in a live Figma link so you can see exactly how it will look and feel — not a static PDF. Nothing moves to development until you've genuinely signed off. We never start building a site that you're not excited about.

4

Development & Content Build

With design approved, we build the site on a staging environment — a private preview URL where you can review the full site before it goes anywhere near Google. We populate all content, configure SEO title tags and meta descriptions, set up analytics tracking, add schema markup, run speed tests against Google's Core Web Vitals benchmarks, and cross-browser test on Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. You'll receive a staging link and a checklist of everything that's been built and tested.

5

Review, Final Amends & Launch

You review the staging site with your team, flag any final changes, and give us the green light. We then handle all the technical details of going live: domain connection, DNS propagation, SSL certificate, Google Search Console property setup, XML sitemap submission, and a post-launch crawl to verify everything is indexing correctly. Your site goes live with zero downtime on your current site during the transition.

6

Handover, Training & Ongoing Support

After launch we provide a CMS training session (recorded so your team can reference it later), a full handover document with all login details and documentation, and 30 days of priority support for any questions or small tweaks. If you're on a care plan, we take it from there — monitoring, updating, and growing your site month by month. If you're not, we're always available for ad-hoc work when you need us.

▾

Will my new website rank on Google?
▾
Every website we build has strong SEO foundations from day one — clean code, proper heading structure, optimised meta tags, schema markup, Core Web Vitals compliance, and local SEO signals. However, ranking for competitive terms like "web design Wollongong" or "plumber Wollongong" typically requires ongoing SEO work — link building, content development, and Google Business Profile optimisation — over 3–6 months. Can you redesign my existing website without losing my Google rankings?
▾
Yes — and protecting your existing rankings is a core part of how we manage redesign projects. Before starting, we audit your current site to identify which pages are ranking and for what keywords. During development we map all existing URLs and implement 301 redirects so that any link equity your current site has built passes to the new site. We also re-submit your sitemap to Google Search Console immediately after launch. Done correctly, a redesign should improve your rankings over time, not hurt them.
Will I be able to update my website myself?
▾
Yes. We build every website with a user-friendly CMS — typically WordPress — so you can update text, images, blog posts, team pages, and pricing without touching code. Do you provide website hosting in Australia?

What is the difference between a website redesign and a new website?
▾
A new website starts from scratch — new domain, new structure, new content, no existing rankings to protect. A redesign rebuilds an existing website, keeping the same domain and URLs where possible, migrating existing content, and carefully managing redirects to preserve Google rankings. If your current site has any organic search presence at all, a redesign approach is almost always better than starting from scratch — you retain the domain authority your site has built up over time.
