The Problem

Why Most Wollongong Businesses Waste Their Google Ads Budget

Google Ads is the fastest way to get in front of customers who are actively searching for what you sell. Done well, it delivers a measurable, predictable return — often the lowest cost-per-lead of any paid channel. Done badly, it burns through thousands of dollars a month with nothing to show for it.

The difference between a well-run and a poorly-run Google Ads account is enormous. We audit a lot of accounts for businesses switching to us, and the same mistakes appear every time.

🎯

Targeting too broad

Bidding on "plumber" instead of "emergency plumber Wollongong" means paying for clicks from people in Sydney, clicks from people just researching, and clicks from people who want something you don't offer. Precise geo-targeting and match types matter enormously.

🚫

No negative keywords

Without a comprehensive negative keyword list, Google will show your plumbing ad to people searching "plumber meme", "DIY plumber", or "plumber salary". Every one of those clicks costs you real money. Negative keyword management is a full-time job most business owners don't have time for.

📄

Sending traffic to the homepage

Your homepage is designed for everyone. An ad for "emergency plumber Wollongong" should land on a page specifically about emergency plumbing in Wollongong — with one clear action. Sending paid traffic to a generic homepage kills conversion rates.

📊

Tracking clicks, not conversions

Clicks tell you who visited. Conversions tell you who called, submitted a form, or bought something. Without conversion tracking properly configured, you're flying blind — unable to tell which campaigns, keywords, and ads are actually generating business.

🤖

"Set and forget" by the agency

Google Ads requires active management — regular bid adjustments, search term analysis, ad copy testing, Quality Score improvements, audience refinement. Accounts that aren't actively managed every week degrade in performance as Google's algorithm shifts and competitors adjust their bids.

What We Manage

Google Ads Campaign Types for Wollongong Businesses

Not all Google Ads are created equal. We recommend the right campaign mix based on your goals, industry, and budget — not what's easiest to set up.

🔍

Search Ads

Text ads that appear when someone actively searches for your product or service on Google. The highest-intent ad format — you're reaching people in the exact moment they're looking for what you sell. The foundation of almost every campaign we manage for Wollongong businesses.

Best for: leads, calls, enquiries
📍

Local Service Ads

Google's pay-per-lead format for service businesses — you only pay when someone contacts you directly through the ad. Shown above standard search ads with a "Google Guaranteed" badge. Ideal for trades, healthcare, legal, and professional services in the Wollongong area.

Best for: trades & service businesses
🛒

Performance Max & Shopping

Product-based ads that show your inventory directly in Google Search, Google Shopping, YouTube, and Display — with images, prices, and product names. For Wollongong eCommerce businesses looking to drive online sales across the full Google ecosystem.

Best for: eCommerce & product sales
🔄

Remarketing

Ads that follow people who visited your website but didn't convert. A visitor who saw your services but didn't call is warm — remarketing keeps you front-of-mind as they browse other sites, making them far more likely to come back and enquire. Strong ROI for established Wollongong businesses with decent website traffic.

Best for: re-engaging warm visitors
📺

Display Ads

Visual banner ads across the Google Display Network — millions of websites, apps, and YouTube. Not for generating immediate leads, but excellent for building brand awareness in the Wollongong market, supporting a launch, or staying visible to your target audience over time.

Best for: brand awareness
▶️

YouTube Ads

Video ads served on YouTube before, during, or alongside content relevant to your audience. Effective for businesses with a story to tell — a renovation company showing transformations, a healthcare provider building trust, or a retailer launching a new product to the Illawarra market.

Best for: brand storytelling

Month 1

Audit, Strategy & Campaign Build

Every campaign starts with a thorough audit of your existing Google Ads account (if you have one) or a competitor intelligence review (if you're starting fresh). We look at what your Wollongong competitors are bidding on, what their ads say, and where their landing pages send traffic — then we build a strategy designed to out-compete them on the terms that matter.

The campaign build itself covers campaign structure, ad groups, ad copy (three to five variations per group for A/B testing), extensions (sitelinks, callouts, call extensions, location extensions), and conversion tracking. Landing Pages

Dedicated Landing Page Design

Sending paid traffic to your homepage is one of the most common — and expensive — mistakes in Google Ads. Your homepage is designed to serve multiple audiences and multiple purposes. A paid search visitor who clicked on "emergency plumber Wollongong" needs to land on a page that confirms immediately: yes, you're a plumber, yes, you're in Wollongong, yes, you're available now, here's how to call.

We design and build dedicated landing pages for every campaign we manage. Each landing page is purpose-built for the specific ad group it serves — matching the search intent, the headline, and the call to action of the ad. Message match between ad and landing page is one of the most powerful levers in improving conversion rates, and it's something most agencies skip entirely.

Our landing pages are fast-loading, mobile-first, and built to convert. Click-to-call buttons above the fold, short enquiry forms, social proof (reviews and results), and a single clear action — no menus, no distractions, no other pages to click through to. We also run A/B tests on landing page elements to continually improve conversion rate over time.

Landing pages include
  • Custom design per campaign / service
  • Mobile-first, fast-loading build
  • Message match with ad copy
  • Click-to-call & form above fold
  • Trust signals (reviews, guarantees)
  • Heatmap & session recording setup
  • A/B testing framework

Weekly

Active Campaign Management

Google Ads is not a set-and-forget channel. The search landscape changes daily — competitors adjust bids, new search terms emerge, Quality Scores shift, and Google's algorithm rewards accounts that are actively managed with better ad placements at lower costs. We review every active campaign at least twice per week.

Our weekly management process covers search term analysis (reviewing every search query that triggered your ads and adding irrelevant terms to negative keywords), bid adjustments (raising bids on high-converting keywords, reducing bids on underperformers), Quality Score monitoring (improving ad relevance and landing page experience to reduce cost-per-click), and ad performance analysis (pausing underperforming ads, scaling winning ones).

We also monitor for invalid clicks and click fraud — particularly relevant in competitive Wollongong markets where competitors may be clicking your ads to burn your budget. Google provides some protection automatically, but active monitoring catches what the automated system misses.

📊
Monthly

Conversion Tracking & Reporting

Every month you receive a plain-English report that focuses on what matters: leads generated, cost per lead, conversion rate, and total ad spend. We don't pad reports with vanity metrics like impressions and reach — we report on the numbers that tell you whether your investment is working.

Our reports break down performance by campaign, ad group, and keyword — so you can see exactly which terms are generating leads, which are burning budget, and what we're doing to shift more spend toward what's working. We also include call recording summaries (for call-tracked campaigns), landing page conversion data, and competitive insights where available.

Every report ends with a clear summary of the next month's priorities — what we're testing, what we're scaling, and what changes we're making to the targeting or creative. There are no surprises on your bill: your management fee is fixed and your ad spend is always clearly separated.

What Good Looks Like

What Separates a High-Performing Google Ad from One That Gets Ignored

Most Google Ads are written by people trying to cram as much information as possible into a tight character limit. They end up generic, forgettable, and identical to every competitor's ad. Writing great ad copy for Wollongong businesses requires understanding both what the searcher wants and what makes your business the right answer.

A high-performing search ad leads with the customer's problem, not your company's features. "Blocked drain? Same-day Wollongong plumber" outperforms "ABC Plumbing — 20 years experience, fully licensed" every time — because it speaks directly to what the person searching is feeling in that moment.

We write all ad copy in-house, with genuine knowledge of the Illawarra market. We know which value propositions resonate with Wollongong customers — same-day service, local expertise, upfront pricing, no call-out fees, Australian-owned — and we test them systematically against each other to find the combinations that convert at the lowest cost.

Quality Score is Google's internal rating of your ad relevance and landing page experience — and it directly affects how much you pay per click. A high Quality Score can cut your cost-per-click by 30–50% versus a competitor with the same bid. We optimise for Quality Score as a core part of every campaign we manage.

FAQ

Google Ads Wollongong — Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Google Ads cost for a Wollongong business?
▾
Your total investment has two components: our management fee (from $900/month) and your ad spend (what goes directly to Google). We recommend a minimum ad spend of $1,000/month for Wollongong campaigns — giving you meaningful data and enough impressions to optimise. Most established clients invest $2,000–$6,000/month in ad spend. Your exact cost-per-lead depends on your industry — trades typically $20–$60/lead, healthcare $40–$100/lead, legal $80–$200/lead.
How quickly will I see results from Google Ads?
▾
Most well-built campaigns start generating leads within the first week. Unlike SEO, there's no waiting for Google to crawl and index — once your ads are approved and live, you're in the auction immediately. The first two weeks are the "learning phase" where we gather data and refine targeting. Months 2–3 typically see significant cost-per-lead improvements as the campaign optimises on real conversion data.
What's the difference between Google Ads and SEO?
▾
Google Ads are paid — you bid to appear at the top of search results, pay per click, and your visibility stops the moment you stop paying. SEO builds organic rankings that persist without ongoing payment per click. Ads are better for immediate results; SEO is better for long-term cost efficiency. Most of our Wollongong clients run both — Ads for immediate leads while SEO builds the organic foundation that reduces paid dependency over 12–18 months.
Do I own my Google Ads account?
▾
Yes — always. We build and manage campaigns inside your Google Ads account, not ours. You have full admin access at all times and can see everything we're doing. If you ever part ways with us, you keep the account, the campaign history, and all the data. Beware of agencies that build campaigns in their own accounts — you'd lose everything if you leave.
Can I set a maximum daily budget?
▾
Yes. Google Ads has a daily budget cap at the campaign level, and we set this precisely to match your monthly budget. Google may occasionally spend up to double your daily budget on a high-traffic day (this is normal), but your monthly total will never exceed your monthly budget setting. We recommend a budget that gives you at least 30–50 clicks per day to generate enough data for meaningful optimisation.
My previous agency ran Google Ads for me and it didn't work. Why would it be different this time?
▾
We hear this often. When we audit accounts from previous agencies, we almost always find the same issues: targeting too broad, no negative keywords, traffic sent to the homepage instead of a dedicated landing page, and no conversion tracking configured properly. The result is clicks with no conversions and no data to improve from. We start every new engagement with a full account audit — if we can see the specific reasons it didn't work before, we can fix them. We also offer a free audit of your existing account so you can see exactly what we find before committing.
