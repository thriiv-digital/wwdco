FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Wollongong Web Design

Everything you need to know before choosing a web design agency in Wollongong.

How much does web design cost in Wollongong?
▾
Our websites start from $1,950 for a professional brochure site, up to $10,000+ for feature-rich eCommerce and digital marketing builds. We provide a fixed, transparent quote after a free discovery call — no surprises. Call (02) 4003 4436.
How long does it take to build a website in Wollongong?
▾
A standard 5–8 page website typically takes 4–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Larger sites, eCommerce projects, or custom platforms can take 8–12 weeks. We give you a clear project timeline at the start and keep you updated at every stage.
Do you build websites on WordPress?
▾
Yes — WordPress is our most common platform. It's flexible, SEO-friendly, and lets you update content without a developer. We also build on Webflow, Shopify, and custom stacks. We'll recommend the right platform during our discovery call.
Will my new website be mobile-friendly?
▾
Absolutely — every website we build is mobile-first by default. Over 70% of searches in Wollongong happen on a mobile device, and Google ranks websites based on their mobile experience first. We design and test on phones, tablets, and desktops.
What is local SEO and does my Wollongong business need it?
▾
Local SEO is the process of optimising your online presence so you appear when people in Wollongong and the Illawarra search for your type of business — including the Google map pack and local keyword rankings. It's often the highest-ROI marketing investment a local Wollongong business can make.
Do you offer website hosting in Australia?
▾
Yes. All websites we host are on fast, Australian-based servers — important for user experience and SEO. Our plans include SSL certificates, daily backups, security monitoring, and speed optimisation.
Can you redesign my existing Wollongong website?
▾
Yes — website redesigns are a big part of what we do. We audit your existing site first, then rebuild it with better design, faster performance, and stronger SEO — carefully migrating to preserve your existing Google rankings.
How do Google Ads work for Wollongong businesses?
▾
Google Ads lets you appear at the top of Google immediately for keywords your customers are already searching. As certified Google Partners, our campaigns generate qualified leads at the lowest possible cost. We handle keyword research, ad copy, landing pages, and ongoing management.
Will I be able to update my website myself?
▾
Yes — we build every website with a user-friendly CMS so you can update text, images, blog posts, and products without touching code. We provide a training session at handover. If you'd rather we handle all updates, our maintenance plans cover that too.
What is a Google Business Profile and why does it matter?
▾
Your Google Business Profile appears on Google Maps and in the map pack when someone searches "web design Wollongong" or "[your service] near me." An optimised GBP dramatically increases local visibility and drives phone calls and website visits. We set up, verify, and optimise GBP listings as part of our local SEO service.
How does eCommerce web design work for Wollongong businesses?
▾
We build online stores on WooCommerce and Shopify. We handle product page design, payment gateway integration (Stripe, PayPal, Afterpay), shipping configuration, and eCommerce SEO so your products get found. We'll recommend the right platform based on your catalogue and growth plans.
What is schema markup and should my website have it?
▾
Schema markup is structured data code that tells Google exactly what your website is about — your business name, address, phone, services, reviews, and FAQs. It helps Google display your site better in search results, including star ratings, FAQ dropdowns, and rich snippets. We add LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, and Organisation schema to every site we build.
Do you work with businesses outside of Wollongong?
▾
Yes. While our heart is in the Illawarra, we work with clients across New South Wales and Australia. Our local expertise is a big advantage for Wollongong businesses, but our skills travel well.
